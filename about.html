About Wrap House

Our Story

Established in 2020, Hyderabad.

At The Wrap House, food is more than just a meal — it’s flavor, freshness, and comfort wrapped into every bite. Since opening our doors in 2020, we’ve been serving Hyderabad with a delicious variety of wraps, burgers, sandwiches, and platters — each made with care and loaded with bold taste.

From our signature chicken and buff wraps, burgers, rice bowls, and sides, we’ve built a menu that blends bold seasonings, tender meats, and freshly cut vegetables with our in-house sauces. Every item is crafted to deliver a balance of taste, quality, and value — making us a favorite for quick bites and full meals alike.

Whether you’re ordering from Malakpet or nearby, The Wrap House is just a click away on Swiggy and Zomato. With affordable pricing (starting around ₹150 per order), quick delivery, and consistently high ratings, we’ve become a go-to brand for students, professionals, and families looking for flavorful food that never disappoints.

What Makes Us Different

  • Made-to-order freshness, every time.
  • Signature zinger crunch with our house garlic sauce.
  • Honest portions — we don’t skimp.
  • Halal ingredients and hygienic prep.
